Common Questions

Before You Ask, We've Got Answers

The questions every smart business owner asks — answered before we even get on the call.

Do I need to replace my current CRM or calendar?
+

Not at all. Our infrastructure is designed to plug seamlessly into your existing tech stack — GoHighLevel, Calendly, HubSpot, Salesforce, and more — without disrupting your current workflow. We bridge the gap, we don't break the system.

What happens if the AI gets asked a question it doesn't know?
+

We train your Voice Agent extensively on your specific knowledge base. However, if a lead asks a highly complex or out-of-bounds question, the AI is programmed with a strict fallback protocol to politely take a message and instantly route the full transcript to your team.

Do you cover the ad spend and software fees?
+

No. You pay us for the custom build, deployment, and optimisation of the 3-pillar system. You retain 100% ownership of your ad accounts and pay Meta directly for your ad spend, as well as the raw usage minutes for the AI telecom platforms.

How fast can this system be deployed?
+

Once we complete our discovery call and you submit your onboarding form — including your past sales calls and FAQs — our engineering and media teams will have your custom AI and ad campaigns built, tested, and live within 5 to 7 business days.
